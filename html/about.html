About me

I was born on the 29th of June in 1998 in Belgrade, Serbia. Since my childhood, I always loved computers and playing video games. That's how my interest in the IT world started. I will soon graduate from the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the Academy of Technical and Art Applied Studies, in Belgrade, Serbia. In terms of professional interests, I enjoy Web development. It wasn't something I liked at first, but it grew on me throughout my studies. My goal is to become a Full Stack Web Developer.

Apart from Web development, music is my passion. I've played guitar and produced music for quite some time now. Some of my other hobbies include fitness and reading.
